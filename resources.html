A big part of our mission at Adventure is to support talent from diverse backgrounds getting into the creative industries. We've collected some resources from our network and friends that we feel can provide a real help in this regard.

Lecture in Progress

Want to learn more about the creative industry and the paths into it? Curious as to what types of roles exist? Or just want to know what projects are going on? Free for students, Lecture in Progress is full of advice, insight and inspiration for the next generation of creatives.

Creative Mentor Network

If you're looking for a mentor to help your career, or even to become a mentor yourself, CMN is a great place to look. They are a non-profit that works directly with schools across London, to connect talented young people from diverse backgrounds with those working in the creative industries.

Creative Entrepreneurs

All the resources you need to start your creative business. Being a creative an and entrepreneur are not often seen as things that go hand in hand, and there's not much cupport at the intersection. Creative entrepreneurs exist to fill this gap and provide inspiration, networks, and events alongside great content.

Uptree

For young people still in school, Uptree connects you with companies before you leave education, helps you build a network early, and forges relationship to start your perfect career.
